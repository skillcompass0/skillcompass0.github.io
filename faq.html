SkillCompass is a tech mentorship platform designed specifically for graduating engineers and early-career professionals looking to break into the IT industry. We offer free community Q&A sessions and premium 1:1 mentorship with experienced industry professionals from top tech companies.
Our mentors are experienced professionals working at leading tech companies like Microsoft, Fortune 500 companies, and other top organizations. They have 10-17+ years of industry experience and are passionate about helping the next generation of engineers succeed.
While we primarily focus on fresh graduates and final-year students, our services are open to anyone looking to transition into tech or advance their early career. Whether you're a career switcher, self-taught developer, or someone with a few years of experience looking to level up, we're here to help.
